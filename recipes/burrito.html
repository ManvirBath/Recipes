Burrito

Burrito is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine. It consists of a flour tortilla wrapped into a sealed cylindrical shape around various ingredients. The Tortilla is sometimes lightly grilled or steamed to soften it.

    Ingredients:
  • Tortilla
  • Meat (Chicken, Beef, Pork, etc.)
  • Veggies
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
    Steps:
  1. Toss the tortilla on a hot stove and flip it till it reaches the desired cooked level.
  2. Start adding mexican blend cheese on it and let the cheese heat up.
  3. Add hot, cooked refried beans or whatever beans of your preference.
  4. Place tortilla off stove and start adding the cooked meat, veggies, sour cream, gaucamole and salsa.
  5. Fold the tortilla into a burrito shape.
  6. Enjoy!
